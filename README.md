# Chronic Conditions and Health-Related Economic Inactivity
Repository containing model outputs for analysis of impact of chronic health conditions at age 42 on subsequent economic activity at age 50-54 in the 1970 and 1958 British birth cohort studies.

## Working Paper 
Gimeno L, Bridger Staatz C, Goisis A, Dowd JB and Ploubidis GB (2025) Chronic health conditions and health-related economic inactivity in midlife: Evidence from the 1958 and 1970 British birth cohorts. *MedRxiv*. doi: [10.1101/2025.06.20.25329982](https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.06.20.25329982). 

## Files deposited here: 
- Results from regression modelling (unadjusted, minimally adjusted and fully adjusted models, sensitivity analyses, and stratified models).
